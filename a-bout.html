THE BASICS OF ROLLER DERBY

Roller derby involves two teams skating around a track. Each team has one jammer aiming to pass opponents to score points, while blockers work to stop the opposing jammer. A match consists of two 30-minute periods, split into shorter segments called jams, each lasting a maximum of 2 minutes.

JAMMER

The point scorer of the team. They aim to pass through opposing blockers scoring points for each opponent they legally pass. Jammers must be agile and swift to get through the pack.
